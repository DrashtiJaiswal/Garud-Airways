Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the cheapest flights?

Use our Date grid and Price graph to see the best flight deals. You can also set up price alerts to get notified when prices drop.

Can I track flight prices?

Yes, you can track prices for a specific route or flight and get notified when prices drop.

What are the best days to fly?

Typically, mid-week flights are cheaper. Use our tools to find the best days to fly for your specific route.

How can I change or cancel my flight?

You can change or cancel your flight by contacting the airline directly or through the platform where you booked your ticket.

What should I do if my flight is delayed or canceled?

If your flight is delayed or canceled, contact the airline for rebooking options or compensation. You can also check your travel insurance policy for coverage.

Can I get a refund for my flight?

Refund policies vary by airline and ticket type. Check the airline's refund policy or contact their customer service for more information.
